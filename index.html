About

Style is a form of confidence — I help you own it.

Based in Palm Beach County, I work with women and men who have built remarkable lives and want their appearance to reflect that. My approach is personal, unhurried, and deeply practical — no pressure, no trends for trends' sake.

Whether you're navigating a new chapter, refreshing a wardrobe that no longer fits your life, or simply want to stop getting dressed and start getting dressed well — I'm here for that.

Services

What we can do together

01

Style Consultation

A deep-dive session to define your personal aesthetic, identify gaps, and build a clear direction for your wardrobe.

02

Closet Edit

We go through everything you own — keep, donate, restyle. You leave with a closet that works for you, not against you.

03

Personal Shopping

I source and shop for you — in-store or online — selecting pieces that fit your body, your lifestyle, and your budget.

04

Event & Travel Dressing

Galas, charity events, club dinners, international travel — I build complete looks so you always arrive dressed with intention.

05

Seasonal Refresh

A twice-yearly reset to transition your wardrobe, update key pieces, and keep your style current without overspending.

06

Virtual Styling

Full styling services delivered remotely — ideal for seasonal residents or those who prefer flexibility in how we work.
